Online Food Delivery System using MongoDB (NoSQL)
**Project Overview**
The Online Food Delivery System is a MongoDB-based application inspired by popular food delivery platforms such as Swiggy and Zomato. The system enables customers to order food online from various restaurants and track their orders efficiently.

**Project Description**
This project allows customers to register and log in, browse restaurants, view available menu items, place food orders, make payments, track delivery status, and provide ratings and reviews for restaurants. The application uses MongoDB as the backend database to store and manage all data related to customers, restaurants, menu items, orders, payments, delivery agents, and reviews.

**Database Name**
food_delivery_db

**Collections Used**
**1.Customers**
Stores customer information such as customer ID, name, email, phone number, and city.

**2.Restaurants**
Stores restaurant details including restaurant ID, restaurant name, city, and ratings.

**3.Menu**
Stores menu items offered by restaurants along with item names and prices.

**4.Orders**
Stores order details including customer ID, restaurant ID, ordered items, total amount, and order status.

**5.Payments**
Stores payment details such as payment method, payment amount, and payment status.

**6.Delivery Agents**
Stores delivery partner information including agent name, city, and ratings.

**7.Reviews**
Stores customer ratings and feedback for restaurants.
